About Us

CORE4 ENGINEERS was established in the year 2014. The Company has experience people from the same field ensuring high standard services to its valuable customers. We offer a wide range of services for Engineering, Installation, testing, Commissioning and for operation & maintenance. Our company provides electrical supplies for commercial, industrial, residential complex, and utilities.

The company is fast growing from the time of its establishment due to its capacity of providing customized solution in the field of Electrical. The products & services are carefully selected to meet the vision and mission of the Company. The Services includes Designing, Supplying and installation of the reliable and quality products. Core4 engineers will treat every customer equally irrespective of its size and always takes care of its employees, who it considers to be its most important asset.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission is to solve your electrical problems and to provide the BEST possible electrical service to the customers and we have strict quality and SAFETY controls to make sure you are getting the BEST.

Our Vision is to have labor and management work together lock-step insuring the success of Core4 engineers

Safety - A workforce is proof that we take safety seriously. we lay out rules for job safety to which our employees must adhere. safety tool talks are enforced on all jobsites.

Our Philosophy is understanding our customers’ needs, eliminating the obstacles we have control over, and supporting each other as only a family can.

Principle - Our Four Core Principles are Empathy – Integrity – Trust – Respect.
